  • OctoBuy

    My task was to develop a user-friendly service that combines the functions of buying various items, selling those items safely for the people who simply want to get rid of the extra stuff they have at home, as well for the professionals who want to extend the area of their businesses. The challenge was to provide a safe environment for both buyer and seller minimising the possibility of cheating or scamming.

About me:

Hi! I’m Maiia, a UX/UI Designer, who’s enjoying the process of research and creation.

I’m quite passionate not only about user experience and visual part of the process, but I truly enjoy learning new things and upgrading my skills. For example, for the past year working with Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign, I learned a lot about Graphic design and printing. I’m not limiting myself to one design direction, because I believe that if you are a designer, you could gain skills in many adjacent areas.

Working on a new project, I also try to push myself slightly further to explore the possibilities of the web and what experiences we could give our users.

I consider myself a responsible, friendly and passionate person. Learning something new and working with creative people and challenging tasks is my daily desire. So feel free to say hi and get in touch.
Cheers!
