MED help

UI/UX Design

Project Brief:

Everyone of us forgets to take their medications sometimes. Even when we set 5 alarm clocks, we still could get distracted and forget to take our medication in time. And then we don't remember anymore whether we took it or not. But who knows better than doctors at what time to take the pills ? So the solution would be to create two apps: one app (tablet) would allow doctors to set the time for their patients to take drugs, whereas the second (mobile) would remind patients to take their medicine in time.

Both sides would benefit from it, as those apps have some additional features.

MED help for patients app has the actual drug reminder and all the necessary information about the prescription. Moreover, in order to keep their doctor informed, patients could easily send a report directly to the doctor about side effects that appeared after taking medicine in specific time. And finally, if they've got some other questions, they could choose a doctor from their 'doctor list' and have a chat with them.

MED help for doctors, on the other hand, allows doctors to have all the necessary information about their patients in one file. As well as they've got specific tabs to set time, duration, dosage and prescription for their patients. Furthermore, they could have a chat with their colleagues and patients and organize their schedule in the calendar.

Both apps are intuitive and user-centered. Both patients and doctors could easily navigate and update the information.
