Yogatime

UI/UX Design

Project Brief:

This application was designed with a purpose to unite both yoga and meditation, to design it simple yet useful.

I made two types of navigation in Yogatime App so that user wouldn't be confused by where to click to find yoga courses or meditation itself, how to chat with teachers or other users. Such little things could dramatically improve how the visitors feel and whether they want to use this app further.

Also there's a utility menu so that the visitor could easily find the necessary information: pose description, popular courses, music and information about the teachers.

The blue colours were chosen to calm user's mind down, to remind how they feel when they see the ocean or the sky, to find peace and balance. The orange colour highlights interesting and unusual design.
