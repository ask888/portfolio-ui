Project Brief:

Aperçu is a grotesque san serif type face that was created in 2010 by the Entente. The idea behind the type face was to create a similar style to the classic realist typefaces: Johnston, Gill Sans, Neuzeit & Franklin gothic. It is a well loved font, used by many designers and media across different platforms such as MOMA, De Wiels, Zeit Magazine and the Walker Art Centre.
