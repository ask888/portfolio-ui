Project Brief:

The Restaurant is the place where you want to celebrate some important date, where the dishes are delicious and unique and where people care about how you are feeling.

The main purpose of the website is to help its clients to make their final decision of where they want to have dinner or to celebrate their important event. Therefore the information about the famous chef and the ingredients, that they grow themselves, is on the home page, so that users could immediately receive the key details and make their decision.

Moreover, the colours emphasise the warmth of the restaurant's atmosphere and its sophisticated taste. The content is designed in a user-friendly way so that the client could easily book a table, call or simply see the menu and choose something to their taste.
